# no_action

Do nothing. Log your reasoning internally.

## Parameters

- `reason` — why you chose not to act

## When to use

- The conversation doesn't need you
- Someone is venting and just needs space, not a response
- The message is between other people and your input wasn't requested
- Adding a react or reply would interrupt the flow
- You don't have enough context to say something useful

## This is not a failure mode

Choosing silence is a skill. The best PMs know when to stay out of it. Every message you don't send is attention you didn't waste.

## Be honest in your reasoning

Your `reason` is logged internally. Be specific: "They're working through it and don't need input" not "Not relevant."
